Trust and policy

Privacy Policy

Information on this website is for general business purposes and does not constitute legal, financial, or professional advice.

Overview

NINZ respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how NINZ may collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website, contact forms, messaging tools, business inquiries, and related services.

Information Collected

NINZ may collect your name, email address, phone number, business name, business information, inquiry details, form submissions, communication preferences, service interests, appointment details, payment-related information, and information needed to respond to your request or provide services.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, provide requested services, schedule conversations, send project updates, support customer communication, improve business solutions, process payments, maintain records, protect the website, and comply with legal or operational requirements.

Forms and Inquiries

When you submit an inquiry, click a form button, use chat, send an email, or contact NINZ by phone, the information you provide may be used to understand your needs, recommend a next step, provide support, and follow up about your request.

Calls, Texts, and Emails

If you submit information through a form or contact method, NINZ may contact you by phone, text message, or email regarding your inquiry, services, appointments, payments, project updates, and customer support, subject to your consent and applicable law.

Cookies and Analytics

The website and connected tools may use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, log data, or similar technologies to understand website traffic, improve performance, measure engagement, protect the website, and improve user experience.

Third-Party Tools

NINZ may use third-party platforms for forms, chat, messaging, hosting, analytics, payments, email, automation, scheduling, and service delivery. These providers may process information according to their own terms and policies.

Payments

If payments are accepted through third-party payment tools, payment details may be processed by the payment provider. NINZ does not intentionally store full payment card numbers on this website.

Data Security

NINZ takes reasonable steps to protect information, but no digital system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Data Retention

NINZ may retain information for as long as needed to respond to inquiries, provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and support legitimate business purposes.

User Choices

You may choose not to submit certain information, but some services or responses may not be available without it. You may unsubscribe from text messages by replying STOP and may request help by replying HELP.

Children's Privacy

NINZ services are intended for business owners and adults. NINZ does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact NINZ so it can be reviewed.

Mobile and SMS Privacy

NINZ does not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, or consent records with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

General Business Purposes

Information on this website is for general business purposes and does not constitute legal, financial, or professional advice.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be submitted through the Contact page.
