Quick Answer

What should you know first?

NINZ exists to help small businesses strengthen online presence, improve customer communication, capture more leads, and simplify daily operations through practical business solutions built around people.

Meet the Founder

Nina Dorrough brings communication, operations, and business systems together.

Nina Dorrough is the founder of NINZ. She created the company to help business owners improve customer experiences, communicate more clearly, and build practical systems that support growth.

Her background includes communication, operations, systems, customer service, landing pages, automation, digital strategy, and business solutions. That mix helps NINZ stay focused on what business owners actually need: clarity, trust, organization, and a better way to serve customers.

Why NINZ Exists

Technology should make running a business easier, not more complicated.

Many business owners wear multiple hats. They answer questions, manage follow-up, maintain their online presence, support customers, and try to keep daily operations moving at the same time.

NINZ exists to simplify the digital side of business by creating simple landing pages, communication tools, FAQ systems, and customer support solutions that help businesses create better customer experiences.

Our Approach

Relationships first. Practical implementation always.

We listen before recommending. We look for the simplest path that improves customer experience, saves time, and supports the way a business actually works.

Our approach is thoughtful, organized, and grounded in real business needs: clear landing pages, reliable communication, helpful answers, and connected support systems.
